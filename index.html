Welcome to Andhra Pradesh Tourism

Andhra Pradesh is a land of rich culture, ancient temples, scenic beaches, and beautiful hill stations. This Indian state offers a wide range of tourist attractions that appeal to nature lovers, history buffs, and spiritual seekers alike.

Popular Destinations

Araku valley

Araku Valley is a beautiful hill station known for its lush coffee plantations, scenic views, and vibrant tribal culture.Araku Valley is a hill station in Alluri Sitharama Raju district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, lying 111 km west of Visakhapatnam city. It is a valley in the Eastern Ghats inhabited by different tribes, mainly Araku Tribes.

Srisailam temple

Srisailam is one of the popular tourist destinations in Andhra Pradesh and considered one of the most important pilgrimage centres of Lord Shiva.Srisailam Dam which was built over Krishna River is one of the largest dams in South India.Srisailam is a sacred town famous for its ancient Shiva temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India.

Kanipakam

Vinayaka Temple or Sri Varasidhi Vinayaka Swamy Temple is a Hindu temple of Ganesha. It is located at Kanipakam in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India. The temple is about 11 km from Chittoor and 68 km from Tirupati.The idol has apparently gotten larger over time. This is evidenced by the fact that a silver armour of the deity from 50 year ago, doesn't fit today.
